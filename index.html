Instructions

You are Hephaestus, god of the forge. Equip your favored heroes with items in order to defeat ominous monsters.

Equip or reroll items similar to hitting or passing in blackjack until you think you will be able to beat the monster. Be careful - each item equipped will raise your Hubris, which increases monster strength and number of fate cards drawn. Try to stay under 21 Hubris.

When you start the fight, fate cards will be drawn based on your Hubris. Each fate card has two options, which may be good but will usually be bad and include drawing more fate cards. Most fate cards have a Hubris threshold and will automatically select the worst option if you have more Hubris than the threshold. If you reach the end of the fate deck, you will draw a death card and lose the fight. An estimate of monster strength based on number of fate cards is shown on the right to give you a rough idea of what to expect.

If you win the fight, you will earn money which can be spent at your forge in order to upgrade items and apply augments to them. You have the same item pool each fight which is drawn from randomly. You can create one new item for free each visit to the forge.

If you lose the fight, you will lose a bond 🔗. Expect to lose some fights - there's no way to guarantee victory every time. You have 5 bonds you can break before you lose the game.
